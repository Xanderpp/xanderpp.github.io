We’re reinventing the
construction industry 

Modulous is digitising design and reimagining construction to enable sustainable, affordable homes to be built more quickly and economically on a global scale.

 
 
BE A PART OF THE JOURNEY
 

We believe everyone should have access to safe, healthy and sustainable homes

Our goal is to help eliminate the global housing deficit while improving industry efficiency, designing out waste and raising quality and performance standards.

Designs are based on our proprietary Kit of PartsTM, which is a series of sub-assemblies procured from the established supply chain that can be brought together to create modular homes in facilities local to site.   

It is the first globally scalable model for the design and delivery of homes. 

We’re empowering every stakeholder

We have created alignment between all the key stakeholders in the design and delivery chain.
Our interests are aligned with our industry partners, and theirs are aligned with each other. 
It is a building industry ecosystem.

Our modular Kit of PartsTM has made digitisation possible

We have created a high-performance, productised Kit of PartsTM to standardise the unseen elements of construction. Each element of the kit has a digital equivalent in our software, ensuring price, programme and procurement information is available upfront. 

The Kit of PartsTM is manufactured within the global supply chain, removing the need for fixed factories, and meaning we can engage and upskill local labour to assemble the kit into volumetric modules close to the construction site.
